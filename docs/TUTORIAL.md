# ProVision WorkSuite - User Tutorial

Welcome to ProVision WorkSuite! This tutorial will walk you through the essential steps to get your workspace up and running and maximize your productivity.

---

## Part 1: First-Time Setup (Administrators Only)

### 1.1 Secure Your Account

On your first login, the system will prompt you to complete your administrator profile.

1. Navigate to **Settings > Profile**.
2. Update your **Full Name** and **Profile Picture**.
3. **Crucial**: Change your password to a secure one.

### 1.2 Configure Video Conferencing

To enable high-quality video calls for your team:

1. Go to [ZegoCloud](https://www.zegocloud.com) and create a free project.
2. In ProVision WorkSuite, navigate to **Settings > Video**.
3. Enter your **Zego AppID** and **Server Secret**.
4. Click **Save Configuration**. Your team can now start meetings instantly.

---

## Part 2: Organizing Your Workspace

### 2.1 Create Your First Project

1. Go to the **Projects** page and click **"New Project"**.
2. Fill in the project name, budget, and target completion date.
3. Invite team members by selecting them from the dropdown.

### 2.2 Build Your Kanban Board

1. Open your newly created project and go to the **Tasks** tab.
2. Click **"Add Task"** in the **To Do** column.
3. Assign a priority and a due date.
4. As work begins, drag the task card to **In Progress**.

---

## Part 3: Daily Collaboration

### 3.1 Using Team Chat

1. Click the **Messages** icon in the sidebar.
2. Find your project-specific channel or start a direct message with a colleague.
3. Share files by clicking the **"+"** icon or dragging them into the chat.

### 3.2 Hosting a Video Meeting

1. Inside any chat, click the **Video Camera** icon at the top right.
2. Choose **"Start Call"**.
3. A secure meeting room will open. Team members in the chat will receive a notification to join.

---

## Part 4: Monitoring Progress

### 4.1 Checking the Dashboard

Review the **Dashboard** at the start of each day to see:

- Tasks that are overdue.
- Milestone deadlines approaching this week.
- Team workload balance.

### 4.2 Generating Reports

When it's time for a review:

1. Go to **Analytics** or any project view.
2. Click the **Export** button.
3. Select **PDF Report** for a professional summary you can share with stakeholders.

---

> [!NOTE]
> ProVision WorkSuite is designed to be intuitive. If you get stuck, look for the **Help** icons (?) located throughout the interface for context-sensitive tips.
